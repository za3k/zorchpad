Charlieplexing to control LEDs
    say you have 6 controls. Wire the complete graph (15 undirected connections), with one LED facing each direction on each edge (30 LEDs). You can control the LEDs with tri-state logic. Set one pin to OUT, one to IN, and the rest to DISCONNECTED.
